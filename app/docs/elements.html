Blockquote

Blockquotes are useful when you want to emphasise a piece of quoted text.

Cras mattis consectetur purus sit amet fermentum. Maecenas faucibus mollis interdum.

Julius Ceasar

<blockquote>
  Cras mattis consectetur purus sit amet fermentum. Maecenas faucibus mollis interdum.
  <p class="blockquote-footer">Julius Ceasar</p>
</blockquote>
